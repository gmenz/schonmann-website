Roberto H. Schonmann

Emeritus Professor · Department of Mathematics, UCLA

Home Publications Teaching CV Collaborators

Roberto retired from active teaching effective July 2009. Over the course of his career at the University of São Paulo (1982–1994) and UCLA (1990–2009), he taught courses across the undergraduate and graduate probability and analysis curricula and supervised three doctoral students:

  • Eduardo Jordão Neves — PhD, University of São Paulo, June 1990
  • Pouria Dehghanpour — PhD, UCLA, May 1997
  • Marcia Salzano — PhD, UCLA, April 2000

From 1997 to 1999 he served as Graduate Vice-Chair of the UCLA Mathematics Department.
